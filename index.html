Competitive Team Program

Fly With the Golden Eagles

HD Golden Eagles competes under Athletics Ontario as a member club.

Olympic Development

  • Ages 13-20
  • Training 4 – 5 Days a week
  • Performance Mindset, Academics, Race Modeling, Speed, Power & Strength

Youth Development

  • Ages 8-12
  • Training 2 – 3 Days a week
  • Age appropriate training on Technique, Speed, Agility & Strength

HOUSE LEAGUE & SPEED TRAINING

Athlete Performance Group

This program is designed to work on performance skills like sprinting, jumping, change of direction, and fundamental strength.

House League

  • These athletes ages15 and under will have the opportunity to compete each month in a skills combine to show performance progress. There will be prizes and gifts for athletes competing

Speed Training

  • This program is geared toward developing footwork and reactivity along with speed and endurance
  • Athletes will train and develop skills that will translate to team sports like hockey and soccer
